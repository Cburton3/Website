Rucksack

45-70 liters for day trips. If camping it is neccessary to have straps on the outside to hook on the tent. For half day trips, 20L is enough

Water

At least 1.5lt pp on small trips of less than 4 hours. 3lts for day trips to be refilled. Water purifying pills essential

Skin Protection

Cap or hat essential all year round. Sun cream over 30spf. Sunglasses and lipbalm with over 30spf recommended

Clothing

Summer trips require a thin material that allow for perspiration. Winter trips requires layers that are easily transported

Accessories

For longer trips consider cooking utensils. Bastones significantly help in ascents and decents

Camping

Min 2 man tent for 1 person. 2 people consider a 3 man tent to store equipment inside tent in case of rain.
